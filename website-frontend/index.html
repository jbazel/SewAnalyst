SewAnalyst

Built upon data from WASP, a registered charity built upon improving river quality and safety by monitoring and denouncing illegal sewage dumping, SewAnalyst can be used to generate reports on the validity of these three things:
1. Population Equivalent (PE)
2. Dry WeSather Flow (DWF)
3. Flow to Full Treatment (FFT)
To give you the best analysis of what Sewage Treatment Works are really doing with their waste water!
